Buffett is an American business magnate, investor, and philanthropist.
Buffett is currently the chairman and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway.
As a result of Buffett immense investment success, Buffett is one of the best-known fundamental investors in the world.
As of November 2023, Buffett had a net worth of $118 billion, making Buffett the seventh-richest person in the world.
Buffett was born in Omaha.
The son of Congressman Howard Buffett  he developed an interest in business and investing during Buffett youth.
Buffett entered the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania in 1947 before graduating from the University of Nebraska at 19.
Buffett went on to graduate from Columbia Business School, where Buffett molded Buffett investment philosophy around the concept of value investing pioneered by Benjamin Graham.
Buffett attended New York Institute of Finance to focus on Buffett economics background and soon pursued a business career.
Buffett later began various business ventures and investment partnerships, including one with Benjamin Graham.
Buffett created Buffett Partnership Ltd. in 1956 and Buffett Partnership Ltd. eventually acquired a textile manufacturing firm, Berkshire Hathaway, assuming its name to create a diversified holding company.
Buffett emerged as a textile manufacturing firm, Berkshire Hathaway, assuming its name to create a diversified holding company chairman and majority shareholder in 1970.
In 1978, fellow investor and long-time business associate Charlie Munger joined Buffett as vice-chairman.
Since 1970  Buffett has presided as the chairman and largest shareholder of Berkshire Hathaway.
Buffett has been referred to as the "Oracle" or "Sage" of Omaha by global media as a result of having accumulated a massive fortune derived from Buffett business and investment success.
Buffett is noted for Buffett adherence to the principles of value investing, and Buffett frugality despite Buffett vast wealth.
Buffett has pledged to give away 99 percent of Buffett fortune to philanthropic causes, primarily via the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
Buffett founded the Giving Pledge in 2010 with Bill Gates, whereby billionaires pledge to give away at least half of billionaires fortunes.
